Ensure your match goes perfectly with the help of TeamWhistle

Goals

TeamWhistle lets you keep a count of the goals for eitherteam and see a history of in which minute every goal was made.

Time

On the main time view you can see the remaining time, but also other helpful information such as the next expiring card or the timer for players to get ready for a penalty corner

Cards

With TeamWhistle you can precisely track each card you give with the backnumber, reason and remaining time.

Shootouts

Every shootout can be individually accounted for and TeamWhistle will let you know when a winner is decided.

Workouts

While running a match, your running speed, distance traveled and heartrate is tracked and saved to your personal health collection.

Customization

TeamWhistle has highly extensive settings allowing you to control what vibrations, popups and actions occur.

And the biggest feature of all...

Synchronisation

Users can allow their match to be synchronisaed across their devices, making sure that every action such as pausing the timer or adding a card also happens for other users. Now you never have to worry about forgetting to resume the time or incorrectly adding a card!
